LUCK

overview

Game Of Luck is a 5 rounds game which requires you to select a particular shape, after which obscure cards are displayed, from which you are to guess the shape you chose to seek.

difficulty

The game has two difficulty levels, which determine the number of selections you can make viz; easy: 2 selections, hard: 1 selection.

gameplay

To select any card, click on it. Once a card is selected, it will be highlighted, and the edges becomes rounded. To deselect, click on an already selected card. To submit selection, click the select button.

results

After each selection, you either pass or fail. After the end of the 5th round, the number of rounds you passed are displayed with your remark viz;

  • 0 rounds passed: very unlucky
  • 1 round passed: unlucky
  • 2 rounds passed: quite unlucky
  • 3 rounds passed: quite lucky
  • 4 rounds passed: lucky
  • 5 rounds passed: very lucky
